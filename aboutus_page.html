Who are we?

Welcome to Vorkinsta! At Vorkinsta, we're dedicated to bridging the gap between aspiring professionals and the dynamic world of internships. Founded in 2023, our mission is to empower students and recent graduates by connecting them with valuable internship opportunities that can shape their careers.

Our Vision

Our mission is simple: to provide a comprehensive platform where students can discover, apply for, and secure internships that align with their career goals. We believe that practical experience is essential for personal and professional growth, and we're here to make that journey easier and more accessible.
